संगीत दर्शन

Musical Philosophy

"सुरं वर्णं तु रागो रूपकं तालः क्रिया।
गीतं वाद्यं तथा नृत्यं तुर्यं भरत लक्षणम्॥"

"Melody, color, raga, form, rhythm, action - Song, instruments, and dance are the characteristics of music according to Bharata."

Music is not just performance; it is spiritual practice, emotional expression, and cultural preservation. Through the eternal tradition of Bharatiya Shastriya Sangeet, we connect with the divine consciousness that flows through every raga, every taal, every note.

भाव (Bhava)
